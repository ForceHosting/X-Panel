Privacy Policy

What data do we collect from you browsing our website?

If you use our free hosting, we collect the following information

- Your email address

- Your discord username and tag

- Your discord Profile Picture

- The discord guilds you are in

- Your country you access the site from

- Recorded IP address upon last signin / signup

How do we use your information?

We use your information to prevent fraudulent purchases, manage your account, and to provide better services to our customers.

Where is all the customer data stored?

Your customer data is stored in a high security database only accessible by our system administrators team and cannot be connected outside of a secured proxy.

User Rights.

At any point in time you can do these things.

- Withdraw your data from our database

- Request a copy of your customer data

- Request us to restrict us from collecting some of your data

How do we secure your data.

Most of your data is stored with discord e.g. discord email. Data that is stored by us is in a firewall and only accessible by the system administrators.

CPPA

The CPPA is designed to protect any US citizens data, you have the right to visit our website anonymously and visit our privacy policy page.
